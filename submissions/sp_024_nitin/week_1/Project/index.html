Donald Duck

A Temperamental, comical cartoon character

Donald Fauntleroy Duck is a cartoon character created in 1934 at Walt Disney Productions. Donald is an anthropomorphic white duck with a yellow-orange bill, legs, and feet. He typically wears a sailor shirt and cap with a bow tie. Donald is most famous for his semi-intelligible speech and his mischievous and temperamental personality. Donald Duck was created by the Late Walt Disney (1901-1966) and made its first appearance in "The Wise Little Hen" in 1934. Donald is most closely associated with Mickey Mouse and shares a friendly rivalry relationship with him. Donald Duck also appeares several times along with other Walt Disney creations such as Goofy, his nephews Huey, Dewey and Louie, Goofy's pet Pluto and others. Daisy Duck is Donald's wife/girlfriend/lover in many of Donald Duck's cartoons. Donald also occationally appears in programs of other of Walt Disney creations from time to time in major or minor roles
